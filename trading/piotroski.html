Piotroski Method

This method uses different financial ratios generated from the financial statements of a company to generate an F score on the basis of which a trading strategy is created.
The following is the link to the paper describing the algorithm in detail. link

This method generates an score called the F-score using 9 ratios belonging to 3 groups namely, Profitability, Leverage and Liquidity, Operating Efficiency

Profitability

  1. Return on Assets
  2. Operating Cash Flow
  3. Change in ROA
  4. Accruals

Leverage and Liquidity

  1. Change in Leverage
  2. Change in Current Ratio
  3. Change in no. of Shares

Operating Efficiency

  1. Change in Gross Margin
  2. Change in Asset Turnover Ratio
