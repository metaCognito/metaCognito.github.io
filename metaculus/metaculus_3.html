Moving onto the second method where I consulted the news related to the IPO. Various sources which I shall add on here gave estimates from $4.5B to $6.5B for the valuation of Reddit.

Since the question asks what the value will be after one day of trading, we refer to an article that shows us the results of many companies after one day of their IPOs

Using these two datasets we can generate two random variables X and Y. X refers to the initial valuation and Y refers to the multiplying factor post day 1.

Estimating X

Our initial range for X was $4.5B to $6.5B, however since the company is looking for a $5B valuation, we can shift our estimate(or lower it) to be closer to 5.

We assume a uniform distribution from $4.4B to $6B, and linearly taper off from both ends to $4B and $6.4B respectively. This is our estimated probability density function(pdf) for X
